Tokyo:

This is a photo of Shibuya Crossing, a popular tourist attraction for first-time travelers to Japan. It is one of the busiest crossings in all of Japan, fulled with bright and lively buildings. Shibuya Crossing is most similar to New York's Times Square.

What's great about the city:

  • Easy transportation (subways, trains)
  • Very clean and self-disciplined
  • Cultural foods
  • Historical attractions such as the Meiji Shrine and Tokyo Skytree
